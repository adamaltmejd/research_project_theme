This project examined privacy-preserving techniques for medical data sharing.

<!--more-->

## Summary

We developed a framework for sharing anonymized patient data between healthcare institutions while maintaining strict privacy guarantees.

## Key Findings

1. Differential privacy can be effectively applied to medical records
2. Utility of data remains high even with strong privacy protection
3. Implementation costs are manageable for most healthcare systems
