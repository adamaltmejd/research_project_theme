## Research Summary

This completed study analyzed movement patterns in major metropolitan areas to understand commuting behaviors and identify opportunities for improved public transportation.

## Findings

- Peak commute times have shifted post-pandemic
- Micro-mobility adoption has increased significantly
- Multi-modal transportation is becoming more common
