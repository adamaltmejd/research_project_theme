This project investigates the integration of renewable energy sources into existing power grids.

<!--more-->

## Overview

We are developing models to optimize the integration of solar and wind power into national electricity grids while maintaining grid stability.

## Key Areas

1. Grid stability analysis
2. Energy storage solutions
3. Demand-side management
4. Smart grid technologies

## Expected Impact

More efficient and reliable renewable energy systems for sustainable future.
